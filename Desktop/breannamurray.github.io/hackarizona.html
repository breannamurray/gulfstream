This past weekend, I made it out to Tucsan, AZ to attend HackArizona at the University of Arizone. I made up my mind before I even got to the campus that I would not be competiting in the competition. Since it was my very first hackathon, I decided to just enjoy the event and attend as many workshops as possible.

A photo posted by Hack Arizona (@hackarizona) on Jan 23, 2016 at 8:55am PST

In addition to attending workshops, I also managed to do all of the fun things like playing with the therapy dogs and unwinding with some yoga! All in all, despite not working on a project, I learned a lot from the hackathons. Even more importantly, I left inspired and ready to hack away. Needless to say, I had a blast!
