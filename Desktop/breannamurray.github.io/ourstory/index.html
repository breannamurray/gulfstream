Our Story


What if buying healthcare was as easy as buying everything else?

Think about it. We live in a time where almost any purchase is just a click away – like a large battle robot, for example (seriously, look it up). The saddest part is that you’re more likely to get better customer service buying that robot than you are for a service that helps keep you alive and well. Knowing that, there’s really no logical reason why paying for healthcare needs to be as confusing, frustrating, and overwhelming as it is. That’s where we come in.

We founded Impact Health because we want to use data and personalization to bring more transparency to healthcare. We first used our algorithm to help our friend figure out why his costly employer insurance didn’t cover his medications or doctors. Our assessment determined that it was actually better for him to switch to a different plan that met his individual needs, saving him $9,000. No one should be surprised by their healthcare costs, and we’re committed to helping every person know exactly what they’re getting out of their healthcare plan.
